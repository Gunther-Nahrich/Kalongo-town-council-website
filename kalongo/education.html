Education services

Kalongo is blessed with a lot of learning institutions ranging from kindergarten to tertiary institutions, provided below are some of the subsections providing the details of the learning institutions we have in place;

  1. tertiary institutions

    1. Oret Technical institute

      This is located in oret in Kamon Ojwi still within Kalongo town council and they provide technical education services like carpentry and joinery, motor vehicle mechanics, electrical installation and electronics engineering among other technical learning programmes

    2. St. Mary's Midwifery School

      This is located right in Dr. Ambrosoli Memorial Hospital in Kalongo Town Council.

      they provide certificate and diploma courses in midwifery training and it has greatly been of help to the hospital in providing the man power to run the hospital.

  2. Seconday schools

      Below is the list of all the secondary schools in Kalongo in no particular order

    • St. Charles Lwanga secondary school

      This is the first and most known to earlier residents of Kalongo since it started long time ago.

      It is a government school located in Kubwor west, Kubwor, Kalongo Town council.

    • St. Francis High School Kalongo

      This is a private Secondary school located in along Pader road, providing quality and affordable education to the greatest population in the place.

      The management can be contacted on the addresses which are yet to be shared on this site, please check out another time and we assure you it would be updated.

    • St. Mary's College Kalongo

      This is a private secondary school located along the road leading to the hospital

      this school was started in 2018 initially located in Akado before it was shifted to Mission parish adjacent to Dr. Ambrosoli memorial primary school's boarding section.

  3. Primary schools

    Besides the government and missionary founded schools, there is a rising number of private schools in Kalongo and the following are pieces of information on the primary schools in the place and in no particular order:

    • Dr. Ambrosoli memorial Primary school

      this is one of the earliest schools created in the legacy and beloved memory of Dr. Ambrosoli who served with passion at the missionary hospital Kalongo.

    • St. Theresa Girls' school

      this is the only single sexed school known at the time of this publication, it has provided primary education to most of the prominent women, meaning most of the educated elderlies and mothers went to this school, it is found within the mission parish and just adjacent to Dr. Ambrosoli memorial primary school.

    • Kalongo Primary school

      this is the main government founded school in Kalongo where great men and women were nutured to great professional positions.

      it is located in then mission ward and just adjacent to Dr. Ambrosoli memorial primary school, they have been great rivals in primary leaving examinations for a good number of years already.

    • Nimaro primary school

      This is another government founded school located along patongo road

    • St. Peter's primary School

      This is another government founded primary school located along kitgum road

    • King's way kindergaten and primary school

      This is the latest entry started in the late 2010s and it has both primary and kindergarten sections.

      It is located in Akado in Kalongo Town council
